2023

Between 8th-30th July 2023, the ninth batch of EOP was ordinated at Wat Phra Dhammakaya Benelux and trained by experienced teaching monks at Wat Dhammakaya Korsør Lystskov.

2022

Between 3rd-19th June 2022, the eighth batch of EOP was trained under the guidance of several experienced teaching monks at the Dhammakaya Korsør Lystskov Thai temple. In total, ten participants joined to train themselves in the monastic discipline, sleeping in simple tents and practicing meditation intensively for a period of two weeks. The program was a great success this year, and many of the participants have indicated they would like the program to be held longer and would not want to miss it next year.

2019

20 people took part in the program which took place over 4 weeks, between 17th June and 14th July 2019.

2018

The program took place over two weeks, starting on the 4th June, and ending on the 15th June 2018. A total of 25 people took part from 10 countries all over europe.
