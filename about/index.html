Learn more about the European Ordination Programme

Can I join?

The ordination programme is open to everyone of all races, ethnicities and religious backgrounds.

However you must be:
  • Male
  • Over the age of 20
  • Have good health
  • Have good mental health

Location

The ordination programme takes place at Wat Phra Dhammakaya Benelux, Ronkenburgstraat 25, 9340 Lede, Belgium.
