2022

Between 3rd-19th June 2022, the eighth group of European Ordination Program participants were trained under the guidance of several experienced teaching monks at the Dhammakaya Korsør Lystskov Thai temple. In total, ten participants joined to train themselves in the monastic discipline, sleeping in simple tents and practicing meditation intensively for a period of two weeks. The program was a great success this year, and many of the participants have indicated they would like the program to be held longer and would not want to miss it next year.
